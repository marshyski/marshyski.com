Marshy Ski


Marshy Ski, an on-going Indie rock project, sometimes solo, sometimes with friends. The inspiration was formed at such a young age; it was hard to take Marshy Ski hundred percent active. More time and mature-ness ended up landing me a chance to accomplish what I originally started years ago. Playing from just acoustic to adding friends I finally started seeing progress to something more then the music I enjoyed, I seen Marshy Ski come alive. Taking the music that motivated me the most and style I developed playing guitar landed me right where I wanted from three years ago. The only thing that could stop Marshy Ski from happening would be death.
